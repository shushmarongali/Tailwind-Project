Chairman's Message

Dr.K.V.V.Satyanarayana Raju, MLC, AP Legislative Council.

There is no gainsaying, education is the essential foundation to build a strong and self reliant nation Despite being blessed with abundant natural resources, economic prosperity eludes us.

Despite being endowed with most hands to work, economic growth evades us.The reason is not far to seek. We have failed to develop a rich base of resourceful human resource a la the progressive countries. Knowledge is the basis for progress. Efficiency is the key to prosperity. And the source of both of which is sound education. That is precisely the mission of Chaitanya Group. In the pursuit of that mission, we have come quite a long way. But then, our sight is directed not backward and downward but forward and upward. We are not content with past glories but excited about future possibilities. As ever, we forge ahead to get even closer to realizing our mission”.
